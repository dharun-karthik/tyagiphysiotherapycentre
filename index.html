Welcome to Tyagi Physiotherapy Centretherapy Clinic

At Tyagi Physiotherapy Centre, we are committed to delivering high-quality physiotherapy care that helps you regain movement, manage pain, and improve your overall quality of life. Our clinic, located Opposite City Hospital, Beside Old Appalwar Eye Hospital, Dhanora Road, Gadchiroli, Maharashtra - 442605, India, offers a wide range of physiotherapy services for people of all ages and conditions. We also extend our services through home visits for those unable to visit the clinic, ensuring that everyone receives the care they need in the most convenient way possible.

Services

What We Offer

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

We treat injuries and disorders related to muscles, bones, and joints, including:

  • Back and neck pain
  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Shoulder pain
  • Posture-related issues
  • Our treatment includes manual therapy, exercise prescription, and advanced techniques to restore function and reduce pain.

Pre and Post Orthopaedic Surgery Rehab

If you are preparing for or recovering from orthopaedic surgery, we offer:

  • Pre-surgical conditioning programs from fractures, joint replacement surgeries and more.
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation to improve mobility, strength, and flexibility We focus on helping you regain independence and return to your daily activities faster.

Neurological Physiotherapy

We provide specialized care for individuals affected by neurological conditions such as:

  • Stroke
  • Multiple Sclerosis
  • Parkinsonâ€™s disease
  • Peripheral neuropathies and other neurological conditions.
  • Our goal is to improve your balance, coordination, and overall physical function through targeted therapies.

Cardiorespiratory Physiotherapy

Our cardiorespiratory services support patients with heart and lung conditions, offering:

  • Rehabilitation for COPD, asthma, and other respiratory disorders
  • Post-surgery rehabilitation for conditions like coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG) Our treatment plans aim to improve your breathing, stamina, and overall cardiovascular health.

Sports Physiotherapy

Whether you're an athlete recovering from an injury or looking to enhance your performance, we offer:

  • Injury prevention strategies
  • Rehabilitation for sports-related injuries
  • Performance enhancement programs We help athletes of all levels get back to their sports with improved strength and reduced injury risk.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

We focus on the unique needs of older adults by providing care that helps with:

  • Mobility and balance issues
  • Chronic pain management
  • Falls prevention
  • Rehabilitation after fractures or joint replacements Our geriatric physiotherapy services promote independence and improve the quality of life for senior patients.
